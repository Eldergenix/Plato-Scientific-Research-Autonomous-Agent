\section*{S7. Measurement-repair acceptance criteria}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]

\item A biology GoldenTask constructs Plato with the biology domain.

\item Method-signal recall is computed from methods.md and included in summary metrics.

\item Evidence JSONL persists drafted Claim rows before EvidenceLink rows.

\item A claim with no supporting source remains in the denominator.

\item Synthetic rigid transformations are recovered within numerical tolerance.

\item Temporal leakage, duplicate sources, prompt-injection quarantine, and abstention are regression tested.

\end{itemize}

\section*{S8. Interpretation boundary}

The default evaluator executes idea and method stages only. Live LLM, E2B, Modal, hosted PostgreSQL, and authenticated Hugging Face paths require external credentials or services. Same-model reviewer roles are self-critique, not peer review. The temporal pilot is retrospective, and structural discrepancy candidates are not established novelties.
